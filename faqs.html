FAQS

What is CADC?

CADC is the Chinese Association Dance Crew, a collegiate dance team based out of UC Irvine as a constituent of the UC Irvine Chinese Association club.


How do you join CADC?

To join CADC, you must be a full-time undergraduate student attending the University of California, Irvine. Additionally, you must be a registered member of the UC Irvine Chinese Association club.


Do you have to have experience in dancing?

Nope! Many members have not danced before college, you do not need to have prior dance experience to try out for the team.


When does CADC practice?

CADC practices 3 days a week. Hell weeks are held before competitions as needed.
